About Me

I am currently a research assistant at the Institute of Information Science, Academia Sinica, Taipei, Taiwan. My research interest is machine learning (now more focused on deep learning) and its application to speech. I'm especially interested in deep generative models such as Variational Auto-Encoders (VAE) and Deep Auto-Regressive Networks (DARN). My advisor is Hsin-Ming Wang. Before I became a full-time researcher in 2016, I was a software engineer at ASUS, working on text-to-speech (TTS) systems. If you're interested in my work, please contact me through jeremycchsu@gmail.com.
